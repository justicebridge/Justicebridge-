Empowering Migrants Through Accessible Legal Support

A community-first initiative aimed at bridging the justice gap for underserved migrant communities in the UK.

About Us

JusticeBridge was founded by Razaullah Khan, a legal aid advocate committed to making legal information accessible and inclusive for migrant populations.

Our Services

  • Multilingual legal guides (Pashto, Urdu, English)
  • Free legal workshops and webinars
  • Referrals to NGOs and pro bono legal clinics
  • Online and in-community resources
